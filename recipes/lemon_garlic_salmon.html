Lemon Garlic Salmon

Description

Mouthwatering dinner in the airfryer?! Yep, you read that right. This delicious meal comes to you in just under 25 minutes!

Packed with an average of 34g of protein and 293 calories! This is an easy choice when it comes to serving a meal you can love to your loved ones!

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon melted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 2 (6 ounce) fillets center-cut salmon fillets with skin
  • 1/4 teaspoon lemon-pepper seasoning
  • 1/8 teaspoon dried parsley
  • cooking spray
  • 3 thin slices lemon, cut in half

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 390 degrees F
  2. Combine melted butter and minced garlic in a small bowl
  3. Rinse salmon fillets and dry with paper towel. Brush with butter mixture and sprinkle with lemon-pepper seasoning and parsley
  4. Spray the basket of the air fryer with cooking spray. Place salmon fillets in the basket, skin-side down, and top each with 3 lemon halves.
  5. Cook in the preheated air fryer for 8 to 10 minutes. Remove from, the airfryer and let rest for 2 minutes before serving.
  6. Enjoy!
