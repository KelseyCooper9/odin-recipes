Lucky's Quicky Chicken

Description

This chicken is great for those who want to spend more time with their families and friends then in the kitchen! From prep to serve in just twenty minutes, this dish is easy-peasy!

This meal will work with what you have! Any cut of chicken will work fine, just adjust the timing as neccessary. If you are looking for a quick, simple, delicious main dish that works well with a variety of sides- you came to the right place!

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 6 ounces chicken tenderloin strips
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons honey
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar, or more to taste
